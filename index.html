About

Caring for the critically ill,
building tools to do it better.

Dr. Soumya Murag is a Pulmonary & Critical Care physician with a passion for bridging the gap between clinical complexity and clear, actionable decision-making at the bedside.

Trained in the management of critically ill patients and complex pulmonary disease, she is particularly interested in the intersection of medical education, clinical reasoning, and digital tools that make evidence-based medicine more accessible.

When not in the ICU or clinic, she writes about medicine, critical thinking, and the human side of healthcare.
